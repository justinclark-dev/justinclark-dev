My developer story

I'm originally a self-taught programmer, and over the years I’ve learned many new technologies and programming languages, enough so that I can get up to speed pretty quickly on any new technology or language. Still, I feel that to be the best developer I can be, and to be competitive, it’s really best to have a well-rounded education that is complete with a degree. So, currently I'm a full-time student and will finish my Associate's Degree in Programming this fall. After I get my AA I'll be starting on a Bachelor’s Degree in Application Development here at North Seattle College, which is thankfully a fully night/hybrid program. Woohoo... no more day classes!
