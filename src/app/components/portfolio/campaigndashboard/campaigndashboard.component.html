Campaign Dashboard

A reporting dashboard for monitoring advertising campaign performance, and generating revenue & billing data for cross-system data integrations. The Campaign Dashboard was used by account managers from ABC, Disney, and ESPN for monitoring their campaigns.

*Proprietary website, not available for showing.

About this Project

This project was the culmination of my 11 years working for Walt Disney. I actually started off as an advertising reporting analyst, manually compiling a small handful of spreadsheet reports a day. One day, I found out about recording macros in Excel and how I could view the code, let alone manipulated it.

Long story short, over the course of 11 years I taught myself how to program, to be developer, a designer, and all-around tech junkie. Along the way, I helped turn a highly manual ad reporting process into a multi-server, multi-language, ad reporting web application complete with automated UI deployments.

I was the co-developer of this system and my main focuses were front-end, UX/UI, security, and server administration. Secondarily, I was focused on supporting backend services and business logic.
